Instructions

Correctly guess as many anagrams of countries of the world as possible.

  • On the following page, an anagram will appear and you must enter your answer in the text field below.
  • To help you out, the two letter country code will be displayed below.
  • If you want to reveal the answer, then you can click the REVEAL button which will reveal the answer and then you can click NEXT to move onto the next anagram.
  • Once you feel ready, press START and you will be taken to the following screen where the game will start immediately.
